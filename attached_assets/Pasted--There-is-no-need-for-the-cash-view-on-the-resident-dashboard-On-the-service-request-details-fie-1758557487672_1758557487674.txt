тАОWhen Local Food runs is selected, the list of available market is shown on a grid format called Market place. A search field and filters for location and size of store (which is determined by the numbers of item in the store created by the admin) is available to help the user narrow down their expectations. The users can pick any that pique their interest. Once they are in the selected market place, the user has visibility of the items in that marketplace as well as the price and its availability (sold out). The user selects items and they are placed in a cart. When he is done shopping, he goes to his cart where he has visibility of his selection. He can decide to remove from or add to the selected. He also has visibility of the summary of his cost and options to proceed to check out, go back to shopping or leave the page . After he is satisfied with what he wants, he pays to City Connect the cost of the selected item via flutterwave. The user is able to receive a receipt and the progress of the item delivery is shown in a page called Orders. The user can also track his order from the page. When the item is delivered to the user, the delivery man confirms he has given the item to the users and the user confirms on the order page that he has received it. He also give it a ratings and select or type in the reason for his rating if the rating is less than 5/5. The whole marketplace is created by the admin who creates a market for food runs, add items and their prices alongside the discount if he chooses.
